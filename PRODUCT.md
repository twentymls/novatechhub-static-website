# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Italian SMB owners, startup founders, and marketing/operations leads (PMI, retail, services, B2B) who need a digital partner: a website, e-commerce, mobile app, custom management software, performance marketing, or analytics setup. They arrive evaluating whether CatalisiLab is competent and trustworthy enough to hand a real project to. They are often non-technical decision-makers comparing a few agencies, reading on desktop or mobile, and want to quickly understand "can these people do my thing, and are they the kind of team I'd want to work with?"

## Product Purpose

CatalisiLab is a Palermo-based digital studio that combines development, design, digital marketing, advertising, AI, and analytics under one team. The site exists to win qualified leads: turn a visitor into a contact-form submission or a call. Success is a steady flow of relevant inquiries from businesses whose projects fit the studio's range, plus organic discovery through the blog and service pages. Every page should move a prospect toward "let's talk."

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, technical. The studio is a real team of people in Sicily who do exacting work, and the surface should read like that: dark, deliberate, quietly authoritative. Confident without hype, expert without jargon. The voice is direct and concrete, never a vendor brochure. "Built in Sicilia, shipped ovunque" captures the tone: proud, local, plain-spoken.

The human element comes through plain-spoken Italian copy and local pride, not through softening the dark, precise visual identity. Approachability is expressed as "we are easy to talk to and we explain things clearly," not as warmth-by-decoration. Copy at the conversion points ("Parliamoci", "Raccontaci la tua idea") should stay concrete and confident rather than performing friendliness the visuals don't share.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS template**: gradient-and-cards startup landing, hero-metric template, identical icon-heading-text card grids, buzzword copy. Avoid the cookie-cutter look that reads as "any startup."
- **Cheap web-agency**: stock photography, clipart icons, loud/garish CTAs, visual clutter, "we do everything" noise.
- **Corporate / sterile**: soulless enterprise-consultancy tone, blue-suit stock imagery, jargon, no personality.

## Design Principles

- **Show competence, don't claim it.** Demonstrate craft through the quality of the site itself; the medium is the proof for a digital studio.
- **Every page is a path to "parliamoci."** Lead generation is the job; each surface should make the next step toward contact obvious and low-friction.
- **Quiet precision.** Technical exactness, stated plainly. Plain Italian, concrete outcomes, no buzzwords or hype. Confidence over warmth.
- **Local pride, broad reach.** Sicilian identity is an asset, not a limitation. Lean into it as character.
- **Respect the reader's time.** Non-technical decision-makers scanning quickly: clear hierarchy, scannable structure, no walls of jargon.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text and placeholders must hit >=4.5:1 contrast (watch the muted ink ramp on the dark background). Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for the marquee and any reveal/scroll animations. Italian-language content; keep markup semantic and forms properly labeled (current contact form is a primary conversion path and must stay fully accessible). Ensure keyboard navigation works for the mobile menu and all interactive elements.
