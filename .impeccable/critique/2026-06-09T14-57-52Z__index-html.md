## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Mostly not AI-generated, but trips three banned reflexes. Strengths: dark/paper two-world inversion, typographic discipline, bordered-grid "spec sheet" composition. Three DON'Ts present: (1) gradient text on .hero-stat .num (design.css:289-291); (2) hero-metric template (120+/8+/30+); (3) numbered section markers.

Detector: detect.mjs exit 2, 1 finding — numbered-section-markers (advisory), Sequence 01-06. Maps to .svc-num (lines 197-257, TRUE positive: services aren't a sequence) and .method-step .num (lines 284-329, near-false-positive: real ordered process). Detector did NOT flag the gradient text — design review caught it independently. Browser overlay unavailable (no browser-automation tool); injection correctly skipped. Contrast figures computed from OKLCH tokens, not sampled — verify in-browser.

## Overall Impression

Confident, well-built brand site that designed everything except the conversion moment. The Method section gets six animated rows; the contact form (the one goal) has no success state, no reassurance, and a keyboard focus trap. Fix the form and contrast and it jumps a band.

## What's Working

1. Two-world dark/paper inversion is a real system (design.css:474-507, 565-585).
2. Typographic discipline: one display, one body, one mono label, consistent em -> Signal Blue italic rule.
3. Bordered-grid composition reads as spec sheet, not card grid (design.css:424-426, 525-526).

## Priority Issues

**[P0] Muted-label contrast fails WCAG AA across the dark theme.** --ink-3 oklch(0.58 0.012 85) ~3.3:1 on oklch(0.16) bg, used on nearly every small label <18px (.contact-label, .blog-meta, .hero-stat .label, .footer-bottom, .section-head .mono, .marquee-label). --ink-2 ~5.9:1 passes. Fix: lighten --ink-3 to ~oklch(0.68 0.012 85) for text, or promote label text to --ink-2; re-measure form labels/placeholders. Command: /impeccable audit.

**[P1] Contact form has no submit/loading/success/error state.** Posts to Formspree, no AJAX, no button state, no thank-you, double-submit risk. Drives #1 and #9 failures. Fix: fetch intercept, "Invio in corso", branded success panel + inline error, response-time reassurance line. Command: /impeccable harden.

**[P2] Gradient text on hero stats** (design.css:289-291). AI-slop tell, near-zero gain, transparent text-fill liability. Fix: solid var(--ink). Command: /impeccable polish.

**[P3] Simultaneous ambient motion contradicts the quiet/precise brand.** Float-orbs + hero drift + marquee + per-card spotlights + progress all at once. Fix: keep hero spotlight + marquee, drop orbs past hero; confirm prefers-reduced-motion neutralizes JS --mx/--my tracking. Command: /impeccable quieter.

**[P3] outline:none on form fields removes keyboard focus ring** (design.css:612). Fix: add :focus-visible ring. Command: /impeccable audit.
